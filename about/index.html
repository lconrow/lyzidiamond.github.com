Where do you work?

I just finished a fellowship with Code for America working with the great city of Lexington, KY. We built some things. I like to make maps and teach people about making maps.

Most recently, I have been co-teaching Web Mapping Quick Start at the Community College of San Francisco GIS Education Center with Stephanie May as well as leading the Fall 2014 OpenTrails Course with Trailhead Labs.

In January 2015, I will be starting at Mapbox on the small-but-mighty Education Team, writing documentation and tutorials and generally making Mapbox tools easier to use.

I am also one of the co-organizers of Maptime, which encourages hands-on, beginner-focused geographic learning through chapters all over the world. I run MaptimeOAK in Oakland, write tutorials and resources for chapters to use, and help new chapters get onboarded.
